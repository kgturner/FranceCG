Planting information for experimental Centaurea diffusa plants

Column headings are largely spelled out, abbreviations below.
Tagged = individual plant ID, blindly assigned
Row and Column = location in field experiment
Trt = treatment, either control, drought, edge
Pop = population ID
Mom = maternal plant ID, from field collections
Origin = status of population, either native ("nat") or invasive ("inv")
germ.start = date when seeds were prepared for germination
retag.plant = if new plant was reassigned to that tag due to early mortality, "NEW PLANT"
plant.comment = comment about plant
planting.date = date when germinated seed was transfered to peat pot in greenhouse
